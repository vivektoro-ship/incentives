Create a rule

Fill the steps on the left — the preview on the right updates as you go. We pre-fill whatever we can guess; anything with a we filled this tag can be changed.
1

Who gets paid & what they did

The people this is for, and what they need to do. You fill this in.
1 role selected
✦ A limited-time rule adds extra pay on top of the normal pay, and stops on its own once the end date passes.
2

What the pay is based on

We usually fill this in — using only what we can actually measure for this person.
⚠ This uses a finance target — please check with the finance team before it goes live.
3

How much to pay

A flat percentage, a different rate for each role, or rates by level.
For example — on a ₹50,000 loan, this pays ₹175
✦ Set a rate for every role at each level. Leave the last Up to blank for “and above”.
4

Extra rules (optional)

Add a condition they must meet, or pay a bit more or less in some cases.
No extra rules — this pays the full amount to everyone who qualifies, with no extra checks.
5

When they get paid

We usually fill this in from the last rule for the same group.
